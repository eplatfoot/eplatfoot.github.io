St. Simon Action

Table prize raffle sign-up sheet.

Client

St. Simon Parish
Los Altos, California

Goal

Redesign and rewrite the table raffle sign-up sheet.

My Contribution

I created a new sign-up sheet using Adobe InDesign.

I served on the auction committee for St. Simon Parish School in Los Altos, CA. One of my responsibilities was updating the sign-up sheet for the table raffle. The theme of the auction was Speakeasy. I wanted the sign-up sheet to be visibly inviting so people would want to look at it and hopefully make a purchase. The design was inspired by The Great Gatsby movie poster.
