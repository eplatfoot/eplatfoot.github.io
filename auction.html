St. Simon Action

Table prize raffle sign-up sheet.

Client

St. Simon Parish
Los Altos, California

Goal

Redesign and rewrite the table raffle sign-up sheet.

My Contribution

I created a new sign-up sheet using Adobe InDesign.

I served on the committee for the St. Simon auction, an annual fundraiser for the church and school my family belonged to in California. One of my responsibilities was updating the sign-up sheet for the table raffle. The theme of the auction was "Speakeasy". I made it have an Art Deco style, inspired by The Great Gatsby movie poster. The sheet was placed near the centerpiece of the table. I wanted it to be visibly inviting so the people would want to look at it while they were sitting. I also wanted to make sure it was easy for people to write their names on the sheet so I added lines. There was previously no mention of the prizes they would win from buying the ticket. So, I listed some of our hot ticket items. I also updated the information to be more descriptive.
