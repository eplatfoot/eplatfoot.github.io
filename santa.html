Breakfast with Santa

Flyer/ticket for annual fundraiser for St. Ann Catholic Church.

Client

St. Ann Church
Marietta, Georgia

Goal

Redesign the Breakfast with Santa flyer to make it more eye-catching and convey important information more clearly.

My Contribution

I created a new flyer with Adobe Photoshop.

I volunteered for Breakfast with Santa at our church. I was responsible for making the flyers/forms. The original document was in black and white with a small image of Santa. All of the information was in block text. These flyers were spread throughout the church. I decided to make the flyer more visually appealing, and I thought more kids might be drawn to the big, colorful Santa and ask their parents to bring them to breakfast! I wanted Santa to be a focal point. I pulled out the most important details about the breakfast and centered that on the page. I removed a lot of unnecessary text and reworded it to be easier to digest. The previous version did not mention that this was a fundraiser.
